Idukki

Idukki is a district located in the Indian state of Kerala. It is known for its beautiful hills, forests, and waterfalls. The district is also home to the Idukki Dam, which is one of the largest arch dams in Asia.

Some of the popular tourist attractions in Idukki include the Munnar hill station, the Idukki Wildlife Sanctuary, and the Hill View Park. Visitors can also enjoy trekking, boating, and other outdoor activities in Idukki.
