Trivandrum

About Trivandrum

Trivandrum, also known as Thiruvananthapuram, is the capital city of Kerala. It is situated on the west coast of India and is famous for its beautiful beaches, historic monuments, and rich cultural heritage. Trivandrum is a popular tourist destination and attracts visitors from all over the world.

Top Tourist Spots in Trivandrum and their images

Here are some of the top tourist spots to visit in Trivandrum:

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

The 8th century Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple is one of the 108 temples in India dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Kovalam

A very unspoilt beach which offers a serene atmosphere. Ideal for family and friends to spend quality time on the beach

Napier Museum

The Museum is grounds to the Trivandrum Zoo, one of the oldest zoological gardens in India.
