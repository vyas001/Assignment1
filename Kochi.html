Kochi

Kochi, also known as Cochin, is a major port city on the southwest coast of India by the Arabian Sea. It has a rich history as a trading port, and is known for its colonial architecture, cultural diversity, and scenic beauty. Kochi is often called the "Queen of the Arabian Sea" and is a popular tourist destination.

Some of the must-visit places in Kochi include Fort Kochi, Mattancherry Palace, Paradesi Synagogue, Chinese Fishing Nets, and St. Francis Church. The city is also famous for its delicious seafood, street food, and traditional Kathakali dance performances.
